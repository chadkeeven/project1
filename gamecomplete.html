Carne Asada

×

Carne Asada is a damn fine taco. Marinated skirt steak, typically, but sometimes flank. The steak should be seared a bit. Usually served topped with guacamole, cilantro, and onions. Try the carne asada taco at Tacos Rapidos, which is open 24 hours and has several locations. This one is an old Taco Bell and has the worst ambiance around. Get it to go.

Pescado Asado

×

Pescado Asado, grilled fish, is one of the two types of fish tacos originating from Baja Mexico. It's the lesser of the two but is the healthier option. Try it at Siete Mares.

Barbacoa

×

Barbacoa. Oh man, so good. Barbacoa just means roasted meat, typically beef or lamb. Potentially my favorite. Very messy. Barbacoa is the origin of the food known throughout the world as Barbecue. So delicious, usually served with sliced raw radishes a sprinkle of Cotija, a salty cheese like Feta. Get it at El Trompito.

De Cabeza

×

Tacos de Cabeza are made from the head of the cow, which is steamed whole until tender. Typically what you get is the cheeks. So good. A good base for hot salsa. Try the cabeza taco at Patzcuaro's. It's right near my house,. Stop by and say hi.

Tripa

×

Tripa is tripe - the intestines of the cow. Not my favorite. But very popular. Try them at La Calle.

Al Pastor

×

Al Pastor is my favorite. It's marinate dpork that is prepared like gyros, because this taco was created when some Lebanese folks immigrated to Mexico and brought gyros/shawarma cooking. Served with pineapple. SO GOOD.

Carnitas

×

Carnitas is my favorite. Pork shoulder that is boiled in pork fat. How can you go wrong? Not a spciy dish so I usually add lots of green salsa. Carnitas California is the spot.

Chorizo Asado

×

Chroizo asado is grilled chorizo - a different experience of chorizo than when it's in a burrito. If the chorizo is good, the taco will be as well. Should be spicy. Try it at Taco Mex, which is so busy that they have a separate outdoor kitchen out front to keep up. All the tacos are 99 cents. WAAAAT?

Nopales

×

The only vegetarian taco on this list. Unique to northern Mexico,
Nopales is a type of cactus. It takes a lot of prep, so maybe
not one to make at home. Delicious. Get it at Tarasco's on Feds.
